A molecule of an element is present by chemical formulae.
Which represent number of atoms in one molecule of that element.
For example :-
Oxygen gas has 2 atoms of Oxygen.
Ozone has 3 atoms of Oxygen.

Atomicity :-

Number of atoms present in one molecule of an element.

Monoatomic elment :-

These elements are having a single atom in its chemical formulae.
For example :-
He, Ne, Ar, Kr, Zn etc.

Diatomic element :-

These elements are having 2 atoms in its chemical formulae.
For example :-
Hydrogen gas, Oxygen gas, Nitrogen gas etc.

Triatomic elements :-

These are having 3 atoms in its chemical composition.
For example :-
Ozone etc.

Polyatomic elements :-

These are having more than 4 atoms in its composition.
For example :-
Sulphur etc.
In atoms protons and neutrons are sitiuated in the neucleus and electrons are revolving in the shells.


In atoms no. of p+ = no. of e-.

Electrons are revolving in shells. The outer most shell is known as valence shell.

Number of e- in valence shell is known as valence electrons.
Valency - A combining capacity of an atom is known as valency.
Valency is always a whole number.
